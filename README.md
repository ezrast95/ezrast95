## Hi, I'm Ezra 👋

I'm a PhD student at Tel Aviv University working on machine learning for untargeted metabolomics. Supervised by Prof. Yariv Brotman (plant metabolomics and metabolite-GWAS).

My master's was in untargeted plant metabolomics under Prof. Brotman — hands-on LC-MS/MSn on Thermo Orbitraps (Q Exactive, Orbitrap IQX), mostly on mGWAS in Wild Emmer wheat seeds. I ran the full pipeline from raw files to biological conclusion using Compound Discoverer and custom Python/R.

My PhD focus is developing ML methods for the metabolomics pipeline — spectrum representation learning, spectrum-to-structure annotation, and the bridge between sequence/genomic data and metabolomic readouts. I work exclusively on public data (MetaboLights, Metabolomics Workbench, GNPS, MoNA, MassBank).

## What's here

[**metabolomics-ml-curriculum**](https://github.com/ezrast95/metabolomics-ml-curriculum) is my public learning log during the PhD survey phase: hands-on tours of modern metabolomics ML tools (DreaMS, MIST, MassSpecGym, SIRIUS/CSI:FingerID), critical reviews of each, and small reimplementations in PyTorch.

More polished repositories will follow as the curriculum produces portfolio pieces.

## Stack
